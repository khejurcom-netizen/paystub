Frequently Asked Questions

Are free paystub generators legal to use?
Yes, when used to generate accurate documentation of real wages paid. Using them to create fake income documents for loans or benefits is fraud and illegal in most jurisdictions. Always generate from actual payroll data.
Will banks and landlords accept PDFs from these tools?
Most professional-looking PDFs from reputable generators like pdfpaystub.com and paystubsonline.net are accepted, especially when they include proper company details, tax calculations, and YTD figures. However, some institutions prefer stubs directly from payroll providers (Gusto, ADP, etc.).
Do these tools calculate 2026 taxes correctly?
The better ones (including the ones highlighted) update their tax tables regularly for federal, FICA, and many state taxes. Always double-check complex situations (multi-state, special deductions) with a tax professional.
What's the difference between a paystub generator and full payroll software?
Paystub generators create standalone PDF documents. Full payroll platforms (Gusto, OnPay, ADP) handle calculations, tax filing, direct deposit, and employee self-service portals. Use generators for occasional needs; use full platforms when running regular payroll.
Can I generate paystubs for past pay periods?
Yes, most quality tools allow you to backdate pay periods as long as the data reflects actual hours and compensation paid.
